`public/images/problend/generated/`

## Brand Context

ProBlend is a premium Indian fitness nutrition company built around smart protein shake vending machines. The public site should show a real, physical, venue-ready nutrition machine: green, modern, hygienic, cashless, and suitable for gyms, offices, colleges, residences, and high-footfall venues.

The generated assets all use a vivid electric-green ProBlend machine in dark/premium fitness or venue settings. The redesign should lean into that visual system instead of inventing abstract SaaS layouts, dashboards, fake charts, or generic AI-style cards.

## Usage Rules

- Use generated assets as real page imagery, not tiny thumbnails.
- Preserve image aspect ratios with stable responsive containers.
- Do not add heavy color overlays that hide the green machine.
- Do not crop out the machine unless using a deliberate close-up detail.
- Do not place long paragraphs over busy image areas.
- Do not invent additional product photos or flavour product cards unless the user supplies them.
- Do not use these assets to imply ProBlend sells packaged snacks; the public story is fresh protein shake vending and placement solutions.
